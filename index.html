Yonge Street Mission (YSM)Volunteer Page Redesign

Project Summary

Yonge Street Mission is a Toronto Organization that focuses on helping people living with chronic poverty to rise. It relies on volunteers and donors to continue their work. That is why it is crucial for their website to make sure the volunteer sign-up process is convenient and straightforward.

My Responsibilities

  • UX Designer
  • UI Designer

Redesign Team

  • Isabela Villanoy
  • Vicky Zhang
  • Ann Darroles

Goal

YSM’s volunteer signup page leads their user to take multiple steps and external website input to successfully book their time. Our team’s objective is to increase volunteer traffic to the website, improve the rate of conversion, and to make the volunteer signup process convenient and simple.

Role Overview

For our first meeting I have communicated with my team my strong UX and UI Design attributes. For this reason, after we have gathered the data we need from User Research process, they have given me the liberty to design our task flow prototype and tasks overview. From there, me and my team discussed how we could reiterate it and develop it to mid/hi-fidelity prototype. I was also responsible for creating the Design System/ UI Elements for our prototypes. Working with what is currently established within the YSM Brand, our team has decided to improve on it by keeping their UI Elements consistent. Lastly, we shared the responsibilities when it comes the division of pages within the prototype. To keep the consistency however, me and my teammate Vicky worked on the header and the footer that our team will be using.

User Research & Findings

From our interviews and usability test, I created the first version of our task flow. Which was then iterated by my team(as shown below). To focus on our persona, a busy person who wishes to volunteer, we created 3 potential tasks where our user can successfully sign up for a volunteer position.

IDEATION DESIGN

For our ideation design objective, we decided to eliminate the external signup page by keeping it within the YSM website. It will then give our persona 3 possible volunteer options, but with the same volunteer signup page.
